These instructions are for the use of the DCMA and privacy features.

To use any of the following features you are required to be logged onto an admin account.

To change a policy description simply enter the public page as an admin and click on the change policy button. 

To log a complaint, enter the "Logged Complaints" Section found in the footer. There you can see current Complaints as well
as register new ones.
